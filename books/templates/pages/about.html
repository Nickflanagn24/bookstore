About Tales & Tails

Where every dog story begins with the right book

Our Story

Tales & Tails was founded with a simple mission: to provide dog lovers, trainers, and veterinary professionals with access to the highest quality educational resources and expert guidance.

We understand that every dog is unique, and every owner's journey is different. Whether you're welcoming your first puppy, training a service dog, or expanding your professional knowledge as a veterinarian, we're here to support you with carefully curated books from the world's leading experts.

Our team consists of passionate dog lovers who have dedicated their lives to understanding canine behavior, health, and training. We work directly with publishers, authors, and industry experts to bring you the most current and comprehensive resources available.
